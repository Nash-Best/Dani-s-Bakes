Our Story

Dani Bakes began as a small home bakery in 2010, when Shamillah Bechwa decided to turn her passion for baking into a profession. What started with just a few loyal customers has grown into a beloved local bakery known for its artisanal breads, delectable pastries, and custom celebration cakes.

We still maintain our commitment to traditional baking methods, using only the finest ingredients and never compromising on quality. Every item in our bakery is made from scratch with care and attention to detail.
