About Dani's Bakes

Your neighborhood artisanal bakery crafting love in every loaf.

Founded in 2010 by Shamillah Bechwa, Dani's Bakes began as a humble home bakery with a passion for traditional baking methods. Over the last decade, we have grown into one of Gaborone's beloved bakeries, known for our artisanal breads, delicious pastries, and custom celebration cakes made from only the finest natural ingredients.

We believe in baking with heart and soul, honoring the time-tested craft while adding our own creative twist. Every product is handmade with care, ensuring the perfect balance of taste, texture, and freshness for every customer.

What started in Shamillah's kitchen has blossomed into a thriving family business, with her daughters Sarah and Michelle joining the team. Together, they've expanded the bakery's offerings while maintaining the same dedication to quality and personal touch that made Dani's Bakes a household name in Gaborone.

Our Mission & Values

Our Mission

To create exceptional baked goods that bring joy to our community while preserving traditional baking methods and fostering meaningful connections through food.

  • Quality ingredients, no compromises
  • Handcrafted with care and attention
  • Supporting local suppliers
  • Creating memorable experiences

Our Values

  • Excellence: We strive for perfection in every creation
  • Tradition: Honoring time-tested baking methods
  • Innovation: Bringing fresh ideas to classic recipes
  • Community: Building relationships through our passion for baking
  • Sustainability: Mindful of our environmental impact

Community Involvement

At Dani's Bakes, we believe in giving back to the community that has supported us throughout our journey.

Local Partnerships

We work closely with local farmers and suppliers, supporting the local economy while ensuring the freshest ingredients for our products.

Baking Workshops

We regularly host baking workshops for children and adults, sharing our knowledge and passion for traditional baking methods.

Charity Support

We donate bread and pastries to local food banks and participate in community fundraising events throughout the year.
